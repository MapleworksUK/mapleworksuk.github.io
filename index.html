Great Location

When you visit the workspace you will love it. It is in trendy Maple Road and it is just 4 minutes from Surbiton station, so you can travel to and from Waterloo in under half an hour - especially convenient for people who have come from other parts of the Capital or further afield to make connections and do business here.

Facilities

  • No-hassle internet access
  • Ground floor hub workspace, comfy business lounge, three pods for those private meetings – bean to cup coffee (gratis)
  • Lower ground ideation suite / training / board room – book for an hour or all day
  • Upper floor private rooms for long-stay residents and unlimited ‘all you-can-eat’ hot-deskers, private kitchen with complimentary tea/coffee and patio.
  • Virtual office
  • Postage in and out
  • VOIP phone if you need it
  • We can arrange just about anything else – ask your host.

The Vision behind Maple Works

Everyone needs flexible, affordable workspace in today’s business environment. The cost of an office is often out of reach for the growing army of homeworkers and hot deskers, but people need contact with other people – clients, collaborators, mentors, etc. Companies too need low cost, light touch solutions and plenty of energetic collaboration in order to stay in the game.

The people behind Maple Works are Richard Sealtiel and David Randall. David and Richard wanted to provide a place for professionals to work comfortably near home in this grown-up, connected way, whilst plugging into London’s developing ecosystem of shared workspaces and accelerators.
